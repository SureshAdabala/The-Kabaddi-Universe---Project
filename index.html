Welcome to Pro Kabaddi League

The Pro Kabaddi League (PKL) is India's premier professional kabaddi league, bringing together the best kabaddi players from across the country and the world. Since its inception, PKL has revolutionized the sport of kabaddi, making it one of the most-watched and celebrated sports leagues in India. With thrilling matches, passionate fans, and world-class athletes, PKL continues to showcase the perfect blend of strength, strategy, and agility that defines the sport of kabaddi.

About Kabaddi

History of Kabaddi

Kabaddi is one of the oldest sports in India, with roots tracing back over 4000 years. The sport originated in ancient India and was played as a form of combat training. The modern form of kabaddi combines elements of wrestling, tag, and strategy, making it a unique and exciting sport.

Evolution of Pro Kabaddi League

The Pro Kabaddi League was launched in 2014, transforming kabaddi from a traditional sport into a modern professional league. The league has grown exponentially over the years, expanding from 8 teams in Season 1 to 12 teams currently. PKL has not only popularized kabaddi in India but also attracted international players from countries like Iran, South Korea, and Bangladesh.

League Growth

The league started with 8 teams in its inaugural season and has expanded to 12 teams, making it one of the largest kabaddi leagues globally. Each season brings new talent, intense rivalries, and unforgettable moments. The league has produced numerous star players and has been instrumental in making kabaddi a household name across India and beyond.
